Our Methodology

We follow industry standards (OSSTMM, PTES, NIST) to ensure thorough coverage.

1. Scope Definition

We work with you to define the assets, rules of engagement, and testing windows.

2. Reconnaissance & Scanning

Gathering intelligence and performing automated scans to map the attack surface.

3. Manual Exploitation

Ethical hacking experts attempt to exploit vulnerabilities to validate severity and impact (no false positives).

4. Reporting

Delivering comprehensive reports with executive summaries and technical remediation steps.

5. Re-Testing

Verifying that the patched vulnerabilities have been successfully resolved.
